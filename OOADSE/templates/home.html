Music Generation based on Facial Emotions using Deep Learning

DeepTunes focuses on

  • Facial Emotion Recognition to identify the emotion from the given face in the image or webcam
  • Generating Music apt to the recognised emotion through deep learning
The goal of this project is to generate music, based on the emotional state detected by facial expression, so as to induce a positive emotional response.
